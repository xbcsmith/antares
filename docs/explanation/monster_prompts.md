# Monster Prompts

## Ice Elemental

An agile ice elemental with a sleek, humanoid silhouette composed of swirling, translucent white frost and sharp, delicate icicle formations. Its core pulses with a soft, internal silver glow, offering a glimpse of an ethereal skeleton. Dark, lightweight, almost polished steel armor segments, adorned with subtle crystalline growths, provide protection. It wields elegantly curved weapons of dark, enchanted crystal, their blades razor-sharp and edged with a subtle, icy luminescence, projecting a piercing, cold resolve.

A stoic glacial elemental, its massive humanoid body formed from opaque, ancient blue-white ice, streaked with veins of deep frozen shadow. Its presence is heavy and weathered, with a faint, pale internal luminescence like frozen starlight. Worn, dark metal armor, encrusted with thick hoarfrost and adorned with crude engravings, covers its vital points. It carries formidable weapons of dark, heavy ice, their surfaces rough and unpolished, emanating an intense, chilling aura.

A humanoid ice elemental, meticulously crafted from razor-sharp, multifaceted blue and silver ice crystals. Its form glows with an internal ethereal cyan light, visible through jagged fissures, contrasting with dark, obsidian-like armor plates etched with intricate frost patterns on shoulders, forearms, and shins. It wields a menacing bow and sword, both fashioned from polished dark ice with glowing sapphire edges, its eyes emitting a piercing cold brilliance.

## Basilisk

The basilisk is a reptilian monster. Although it has eight legs, its slow
metabolic proceSs allows it only slow movement. While it has strong,
toothy jaws, the basilisk's major weapon is its gaze by means of which it is
able to turn to stone any fleshly creature which meets its glance. However,
if its gaze is reflected so that the basilisk sees its own eyes, it will itself be
petrified, but this requires light at least equal to bright torchlight and a
good, smooth reflector. Basilisks are usually dull brown with yellowish
underbellies. Their eyes are glOWing pale green. The basilisk is able to see
in both the astral and ethereal planes. In the former plane its gaze kills,
while in the latter it turns victims to ethereal stone which can only be seen
by those who are in that plane or can see ethereal objects.

**3D Model Prompt — Basilisk**

> A reptilian creature with eight thick, lizard-like legs arranged symmetrically along a heavy, low-slung body. The body is bulky and slow-looking, with dense, overlapping scales. The head is large and blunt with a wide, powerful jaw lined with prominent, jagged teeth. The skin is dull, earthy brown across the dorsal surface with a pale yellowish underbelly. The eyes are large and luminous, glowing pale green, slightly forward-facing to suggest a predatory gaze. The overall silhouette should read as massive, ancient, and dangerous despite its squat posture. Hard-surface reptile scales, realistic PBR texturing, neutral T-pose or rest stance suitable for game rigging. No wings. No tail spike. Low-fantasy creature style.

## Chimera

The chimera combines features of three creatures in a monstrous manner.
Its hind quarters are those of a huge goat, its foreparts are those of a lion,
its body sports dragon wings, and it has three large heads. It can claw with
its fore legs, its goat head is armed with two long horns, its lion head has
powerful jaws and sharp teeth, and its dragon head is likewise equipped.
If a chimera desires (50% chance) its dragon head can breathe fire with a
range of 5" and causing 3-24 points damage (saving throw applicable).
Chimerae speak a very limited form of red dragon language.
Description: The goatish body paris are black with amber eyes and
yellOWish horns. The lion-like parts are towny yellow with a dark brown
mane, green eyes, and red mow. The dragon wings are brownish-block,
the dragon head orange, and the eyes and mouth block.

3D Model Prompt — Chimera**

> A massive, monstrous hybrid creature combining three distinct animals into one terrifying form. The rear half of the body is that of a large, powerfully built goat with cloven hooves and a thick haunched frame. The front half transitions into a muscular lion's forebody with heavy forelimbs and sharp claws. Two large, leathery dragon wings erupt from the creature's back. The creature bears three distinct heads side by side on a thick neck: a goat head flanked by two long curved horns, a lion head with a full dark brown mane and snarling open mouth, and a dragon head with a long reptilian snout. The dragon head should suggest the ability to breathe fire — slightly open jaw, faint heat shimmer or ember glow optional. Game-ready, low-fantasy style, neutral stance suitable for rigging and animation.
