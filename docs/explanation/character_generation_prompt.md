# Character Generation Prompts

This document contains prompts to generate a **Character Portrait** and a **Sprite Sheet** in a single image. This ensures consistency in appearance, costume, and color palette between the detailed portrait and the in-game sprite.

## Usage

*   **Model Recommendation**: Midjourney v6, DALL-E 3, or similar high-fidelity image generators.
*   **Method**: Copy and paste the specific prompt for the character you wish to generate.

## Prompt Structure

The prompts follow this structure to force a split-view layout:
> **[Description]**. Split image: Left side is a **[Portrait Style]**. Right side is a **[Sprite Sheet Style]** on a white background, showing **[Views]**. Consistent **[Details]**.

## Tutorial Campaign Characters

### 1. Kira (Human Knight)
**Description:** A young female human warrior, eager to prove herself.
**Prompt:**
> A young female human knight with a determined expression, fair skin, and brown hair tied back in a ponytail. She is wearing polished steel plate armor with a blue tabard and has a sword at her hip. **Split image:** Left side is a realistic high-fantasy masterpiece portrait (Baldur's Gate 3 style) with dramatic lighting. Right side is a high-quality pixel art RPG sprite sheet on a white background, featuring the same character. The sprite sheet includes a front view, side view, and back view, along with walking animation frames. **Consistent details:** Steel armor, blue tabard, brown hair. --ar 3:2 --stylize 250
