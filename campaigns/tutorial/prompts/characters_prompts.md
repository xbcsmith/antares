# Character prompt assets

These prompts are designed to generate detailed character reference images for the tutorial campaign. Each prompt produces a single, clean, high-quality character portrait with realistic detail, strong visual identity, and a clean composition suitable for concept art or later 3D reference work.

## Base style requirements

For every prompt, use:
- DnD-style fantasy aesthetic
- realistic facial detail, clothing, and equipment
- strong character identity and expression
- centered composition
- clean, uncluttered background
- no text, logos, or watermarks
- high resolution and crisp detail
- suitable for character concept art and 3D reference

## Character prompts

### Kira (Human Knight)
Prompt: Create a clean, high-resolution, photorealistic reference image of Kira, a young human knight with a determined expression, fair skin, and brown hair tied back in a ponytail. She wears polished steel plate armor with a blue tabard and carries a sword at her hip. The character should be centered, facing forward or slightly turned, with realistic fantasy detail, a clean background, no shadows, no text, no watermark, and a crisp silhouette suitable for character concept work.
