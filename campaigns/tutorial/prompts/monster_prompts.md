# Monster prompt assets

These prompts are designed to generate detailed monster reference images for the tutorial campaign. The document preserves the stronger existing prompts for Ice Elemental, Basilisk, and Chimera while extending the catalog to match the monsters defined in [campaigns/tutorial/data/monsters.ron](../../docs/explanation/campaigns/tutorial/data/monsters.ron).

## Base style requirements

For every prompt, use:
- DnD-style fantasy aesthetic
- realistic anatomy, texture, and material detail
- strong silhouette for game-ready 3D reference
- centered composition
- clean, uncluttered background
- no text, logos, or watermarks
- high resolution and crisp detail
- suitable for concept art and 3D reference

## Monster prompts

### Ice Elemental
Reference description: An agile ice elemental with a sleek, humanoid silhouette composed of swirling, translucent white frost and sharp, delicate icicle formations. Its core pulses with a soft, internal silver glow, offering a glimpse of an ethereal skeleton. Dark, lightweight, almost polished steel armor segments, adorned with subtle crystalline growths, provide protection. It wields elegantly curved weapons of dark, enchanted crystal, their blades razor-sharp and edged with a subtle, icy luminescence, projecting a piercing, cold resolve.

Prompt: Create a clean, high-resolution, photorealistic reference image of an agile ice elemental with a sleek humanoid silhouette formed from swirling translucent white frost and sharp crystalline formations, a glowing silver core, dark polished armor segments, and elegant icy weapons. The creature should be centered, isolated, with realistic fantasy detail, a clean background, no shadows, no text, no watermark, and a crisp silhouette suitable for 3D modeling.

### Basilisk
Reference description: The basilisk is a reptilian monster with eight thick, lizard-like legs, a heavy low-slung body, dense overlapping scales, a large blunt head, powerful jaws, and glowing pale green eyes. It is dull brown across the back with a pale yellowish underbelly and is defined by a deadly gaze that can turn living creatures to stone.

Prompt: Create a clean, high-resolution, photorealistic reference image of a basilisk, a heavy reptilian creature with eight strong legs, dense overlapping scales, a broad blunt head, powerful jagged jaws, dull brown body coloration, pale yellow underbelly, and luminous pale green eyes. The creature should be centered, isolated, with realistic fantasy detail, a clean background, no shadows, no text, no watermark, and a crisp silhouette suitable for 3D modeling.

### Chimera
Reference description: The chimera combines features of a goat, a lion, and a dragon into one monstrous form, with a goat rear body, lion forequarters, leathery dragon wings, and three distinct heads. The goat head bears curved horns, the lion head has a dark mane and snarling maw, and the dragon head suggests fire-breathing power.

Prompt: Create a clean, high-resolution, photorealistic reference image of a chimera, a massive hybrid creature with a goat-like hindquarters, lion-like forequarters, two leathery dragon wings, and three heads arranged side by side: a black goat head with yellowish horns, a tawny lion head with a dark mane, and an orange dragon head with a powerful reptilian snout. The creature should be centered, isolated, with realistic fantasy detail, a clean background, no shadows, no text, no watermark, and a crisp silhouette suitable for 3D modeling.
